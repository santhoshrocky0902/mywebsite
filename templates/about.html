History

A.C.S. Medical College and Hospital was brought under the ambit of Dr. M.G.R.Educational and Research Institute University in the year 2008. Our University is offering high quality centre of learning in the fields like Engineering & Technology, Dental Surgery, Medicine, Architecture, Arts & Commerce, Physiotherapy, Nursing, Humanities and Sciences, Education, Hotel Management & Catering Technology and Management Studies.

Vision, Mission and Quality Policy

To transform our institution into quality technical education centre imparting updated technical knowledge with character building.

To develop into an institute of medical excellence, imparting quality medical education to students and quality medical care to patients. To create doctors with knowledge, values and principles and to help them attain their highest functional ability. To spread health and to make the nation a healthier and happier place to live in.

The College is well-planned and well-designed, built with more than 4 lakh square feet of constructed area.
